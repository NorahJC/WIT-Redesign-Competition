About WIT Partners

Women in Technology (WIT) is where top technology companies turn to develop their most precious resource—their people. As passionate advocates for advancing women in Georgia’s technology community, we are perfectly poised to help you develop and retain your leaders, build loyalty and diversity within your ranks, connect with the community, and, most importantly, achieve your strategic objectives. Being involved with WIT puts you on the fast track to reaching your goals, whether it’s helping your employees gain critical leadership experience or being recognized for your contributions to the community. As one of our valued WIT Partners, we’ll recognize you in news releases, on our website, through monthly newsletter distribution to more than 11,000 technology leaders, and at our monthly WIT FORUMS—all culminating with recognition at one of our signature events. Every dollar you invest has a measurable ROI; you train your top talent, connect with your community, fulfill your marketing objectives, produce results faster, and do it all within your budget. Our WIT PARTNER program is simple—and completely customizable. Just select at least two WIT programs or events to sponsor from the opportunities listed in Partner Packet. The minimum investment is $10,000 for companies with more than 250 employees; $5,000 for companies with less than 250 employees.

Contact us about WIT’s Partners program at info@mywit.org today.

About WIT

For over 25 years, Women in Technology (WIT) has worked to empower and educate women and girls about opportunities in science, technology, engineering, arts, and math (STEAM) by educating middle and high school girls, and young women in college, and by sharing the stories of successful professional women in STEAM.
